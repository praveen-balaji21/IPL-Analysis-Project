
IPL Data Analysis - Insights Summary
Key Insights :



Rajasthan emerged as the most successful team in the dataset with the highest number of match wins.
Punjab and Chennai consistently delivered strong performances across multiple matches.
Teams winning the toss also won the match in approximately 52% of games, indicating a slight toss advantage.
The average first innings score was around 161 runs, showing that competitive totals were generally high-scoring.
The average second innings score was around 148 runs, suggesting that chasing teams faced moderate pressure.
The highest first innings score recorded in the dataset was 240 runs by Punjab against Chennai.
Powerplay overs played a major role in setting momentum, with aggressive starts often leading to stronger total scores.
Middle overs contributed significantly to match stability and innings building.
Death overs had a major impact on final scores due to accelerated scoring rates in the last overs.
Venues such as M Chinnaswamy Stadium, Eden Gardens, and Punjab Cricket Association Stadium hosted the highest number of matches in the dataset.
Teams batting first showed strong winning patterns when posting high totals above 180 runs.
Player of the Match awards were frequently earned by top-order batsmen and all-rounders for match-winning performances.
